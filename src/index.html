JAZZ IS BACK!

Experience the thrill of Jazz Jackrabbit 2 the ultimate platforming adventure that redefines fun and excitement! Dive into a world bursting with vibrant, hand-drawn graphics and an electrifying soundtrack that will keep you hooked for hours.

Jazz is the coolest, fastest, and most daring green rabbit in the galaxy, as he battles his way through diverse, beautifully crafted maps filled with hidden secrets, challenging puzzles, and formidable foes. With its smooth, high-speed gameplay and a wide array of power-ups and weapons, every moment is an adrenaline-pumping journey.
